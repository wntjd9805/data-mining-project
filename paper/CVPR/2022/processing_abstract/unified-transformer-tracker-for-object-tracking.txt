However, current methods in one tracking scenario are not easily adapted to the other due to the diver-gent training datasets and tracking objects of both tasks. Al-though UniTrack [45] demonstrates that a shared appear-ance model with multiple heads can be used to tackle indi-vidual tracking tasks, it fails to exploit the large-scale track-ing datasets for training and performs poorly on the single object tracking. In this work, we present the Uniﬁed Trans-former Tracker (UTT) to address tracking problems in dif-ferent scenarios with one paradigm. A track transformer is developed in our UTT to track the target in both SOT andMOT where the correlation between the target feature and the tracking frame feature is exploited to localize the target.We demonstrate that both SOT and MOT tasks can be solved within this framework, and the model can be simultaneously end-to-end trained by alternatively optimizing the SOT andMOT objectives on the datasets of individual tasks. Exten-sive experiments are conducted on several benchmarks with a uniﬁed model trained on both SOT and MOT datasets.
