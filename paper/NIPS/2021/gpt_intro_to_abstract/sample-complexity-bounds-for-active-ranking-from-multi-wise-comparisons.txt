The purpose of this paper is to present an introduction to the concept of answer format for computer science papers. The answer format is a tool that allows for the efficient organization and presentation of information in a concise, clear, and logical manner. By providing guidelines and specific examples, this introduction aims to familiarize readers with the benefits and techniques of using the answer format in their own research and writing endeavors. Ultimately, this paper will contribute to improving the overall quality and readability of computer science papers by encouraging the adoption of the answer format as a standard practice.
