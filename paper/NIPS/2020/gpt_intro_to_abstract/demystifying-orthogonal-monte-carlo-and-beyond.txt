The aim of this paper is to introduce the concept of artificial intelligence (AI) in the field of computer science and explore its applications. We will discuss the different subfields of AI, including machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision, and examine how they have revolutionized various industries. Furthermore, we will delve into the challenges and ethical implications of AI, highlighting the importance of responsible development and deployment. This paper serves as a foundational understanding of AI, offering insights into its potential and encouraging further exploration in this rapidly evolving field.
